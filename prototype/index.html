The Anatomy of a Dog

The Ears and The Nose

The ears are one of the most important parts of the dog alongside the nose. The nose and ears are what the dog uses to help navigate itself. There aren't very many variations to the ears and nose, the ears can either stand up pointed or floop over. The nose can either be black or pink, sometimes in other colors too, but very rarely.

The Body

This part also includes the paws, especially since the size of the paw relates to the size of the body. There are three types of body types for dogs, but this part of the dog has the most variety, the body can come in all different shapes and sizes but there are five primary types of the body of a dog.

  1. Extra Small
  2. Small
  3. Medium
  4. Large
  5. Extra Large

The Tail

unlike the body of the dog, the tail comes in only a few different varieties, but and each body type of dog can come with any type of tail, theres no specific type of tail that needs to be with a specific body type.

  • Straight
  • Curly
  • Docked

The Fur

The fur on a dog is another very important part of the dog, the fur helps regulate the temperature of the dog and comes in many many colors and lengths. Each different type of fur is for a different purpose.

Short
typically found on dogs originating from warmer climate areas
Medium
typically can be found in dogs in cooler climates, but can be found on almost any dog.
Long
typically found on dogs in cold climates, keeps them warm, typically dogs with this long length of fur have a shorter coat of fur underneath so that they can shed the longer coat for when the weather gets warmer.
